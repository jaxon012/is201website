Star Wars Films Ranked

Film Ranking


Favorite to Least Favorite

  1. Empire Strikes Back
    • The movie that changed a generation and so much lore, wisdom, action, and emotion to the Star Wars universe. The easy choice for number 1.
  2. Revenge of the Sith
    • Perhaps one of the most quotable movies ever. One that tells a harrowing tale of Anakin's turn to the darkside, and the fall of the Jedi order. Captivating in every way!
  3. The Last Jedi
    • This movie gets a lot of unfair hate. As the only movie in the sequel trilogy that tried to do something new and different, the deep themes and profound ideas found in this film are lost on most. It also contains some breathtaking moments that are among my favorites in all of Star Wars.
  4. Return of the Jedi
    • This is a great movie. The throne room scene and the redemption of Darth Vader make me emotional every time. No one is ever really gone.
  5. The Force Awakens
    • This is a fantastic rehash of the plot of A New Hope, but it's still an incredibly fun and joyful return to Star Wars.
  6. A New Hope
    • The original. The classic! While it's SFX were groundbreaking for the time, we should be grateful that this movie was saved in the editing process and became the universe we know of today. It is a great film.
  7. The Rise of Skywalker
    • This movie was.. amazing, but also lacked cohesion with the rest of the trilogy. Rushed, but still contained some of my all-time favorite moments in Star Wars. I love it.
  8. The Phantom Menace
    • Fans in 1999 probably loved this one initally... Anyways.. Darth Maul is awesome!
  9. Attack of the Clones
    • This one just doesn't really do it for me. I love it though.
