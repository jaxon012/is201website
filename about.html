Family

I come from a family of eight, being the fourth of six kids. I have two sister and threes brothers. My youngest sister is the only one still living at home! We grew up with a lot of animals, such as horses, goats, chickens, dogs and cats. Sports played a big part in our family as well. I also love to watch movies. Who I Am

I am from Sandpoint, Idaho. Sandpoint is a small town in the northern panhandle of Idaho, known for its beautiful mountains, forests and lakes. It is becoming more and more of a popular area for both new residents and tourists alike, in large part due to its winter ski resort and a large lake to enjoy in the summer.

What I believe

I am a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I served a two year mission for my church in San Diego, California. I try my best to live the gospel that Jesus taught in my daily life.
